About Us

MANA Simulations provides simulation services including the evaluation of fluid and material behaviour using Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) and Finite Element Analysis (FEA). The team is based in the Fiji Isles and aims to provide its services in the Pacifika region and worldwide.

  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA)
  • Material Behaviour Evaluation
  • Fluid Dynamics Studies
